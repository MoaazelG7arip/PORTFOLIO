Quiz App with Role-Based Access(Angular)

Welcome to our Quiz App, designed for both teachers and students. To get started, users must register or sign in based on their role:
Teachers can create, edit, and delete questions, manage exams, and publish quizzes using a fully-featured dashboard. Once an exam is finalized, it becomes available for students to take.
Students can access and solve the exams assigned to them, provided an exam is available.
All routes are securely guarded using a Route Guard to ensure that only authorized users can access specific sections—teachers can perform CRUD operations on exam questions, while students can only view and solve published exams.
